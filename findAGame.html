Local places to play

Southern New England has many high-quality poker rooms with numerous events and promotions. Here are a few of the most popular offerings in the area.

Foxwoods Resort and Casino

Mashantucket, CT
  • Largest Poker Room on the east coast.
  • Games running 24/7.
  • Many daily Giveaways and Promotions available.
  • Close Vicinity to New York and Southern RI.
Visit Foxwoods' Website

Mohegan Sun

Uncasville, CT
  • Large, 42 table poker room.
  • Daily Cash Games and Poker Tournaments.
  • Free Food Vouchers given to regular players.
  • Offers cheaper games for players not willing to risk much money.
Visit Mohegan Sun's Website

Twin River Casino

Lincoln, RI
  • Fairly new poker room in Northeastern Rhode Island.
  • 23 tables, with limited choices in game type or size.
  • Only Poker Room in the entire state of RI.
  • The Closest Casino with Poker for residents of Providence and Southeastern Massachusetts.
Visit Twin River's Website
